Is Best Free Keylogger detectable by antivirus software?

Our software is designed to be stealthy, but some antivirus programs may flag it as potentially unwanted software due to its monitoring capabilities. You may need to add an exception to your antivirus software.

Can I monitor multiple computers with one license?

The free version allows installation on one computer. For multiple computers, please check our premium options which offer multi-device licensing.
